God, Earth and Man: What can we know?

One of the word that has generated the most controversy in it's meaning is perhaps the word - God. Some men believe that the word is the name of the ageless creator, while there are some who argue that God is a title not the name of this all-powerful creator, and there are yet the other group who question the existence and nature of this God if at all he exist.
The inquisitive mind or the mind of men in circumstances must have pondered about God: while some raise questions others have simply surrendered to popular views. It is not a problem to have questions, the source of answers is perhaps the most important.
Learning or solving a problem is easier when we move from the known to the unknown.

Earth and Man

Every man who ever lived has lived on the earth. According to the body of knowledge, the earth has certain characteristics:

  1. The earth is reported to be among other planets within a solar system.
  2. The earth is said to have a spherical form.
  3. It is said to rotate both around the sun and on its own axis.
  4. It is said to be made up of 70% water

According to the American Museum of Natural History, the Earth is fit to support human life because it is not the closest nor the farthest planet from the Sun; it is shielded from harmful solar radiation by its magnetic field; it is kept warm by an insulating atmosphere; and it has the right chemical ingredients for life, including water and carbon.
